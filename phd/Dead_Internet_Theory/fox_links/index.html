Bots on bluesky

Regarding bots on bluesky:

One thing I've noticed is people's profiles is this trend of writing "No DMs, they're always scams," or some version of.

Users can easily disable DMs. The settings are "Allow DMs from... Everyone / People you follow / No one."

So writing "No DMs" is unnecessary, and... based on the times I see this type of profile... I'm starting to suspect it's a strategy used by bots to manipulate trust levels.

Announcing that you're trying to avoid scam/spammers is a great way to convince others that you're not a scam/spam account.

But this person here is following 174.1k other accounts (and I literally just watched it jump from 173.9k a few seconds ago). Only 26.6k follow back, which indicates the number of people (or bots) that automatically follow back when someone follows them.

And there is no link on their profile, despite advertising their "small creative shop" in their profile. https://bsky.app/profile/crimeajewel.bsky.social

They're posts are almost entirely somewhat bad pics of jewelry.

And they NEVER reply to people's comments on their posts. Nor do they like/heart comments made to their posts. Nor do they every comment on anyone else's posts.

They post every hour or every half-hour, in short segments of a few hours at a time.

So... To me, this account screams bot. And the "No DMs!" In the profile has started to become an "obvious" sign to me, based on the many accounts I see just like this one.
